About the Platform

Empowering Futures

myThrivehood was born from a simple realisation: too many talented students and young professionals lack access to quality mentorship, career guidance, and a supportive community — simply because they can't afford it.

Founded on April 21, 2021, myThrivehood has grown into a trusted platform under the Dhanam Educational Trust, offering 100% free services to help people navigate their education and career journeys with confidence.

Whether you're a student figuring out your path, a fresh graduate entering the workforce, or a young professional looking to grow — myThrivehood is your community.
