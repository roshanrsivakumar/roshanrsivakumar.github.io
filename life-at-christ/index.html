Life at Christ

Christ University, Bangalore — where ambition met community, and education became an experience worth living.

Overview

The Christ Chapter

Christ University, Bangalore — one of India's most prestigious institutions — became home for the most transformative year of my academic journey. The MBA program at Christ isn't just a degree; it's a full-immersion experience in leadership, collaboration, and growth.

As part of the L3 (Lean Operations and Systems) cohort, I was surrounded by brilliant minds, driven by a culture of excellence, and shaped by experiences that ranged from managing national-level events to winning analytics competitions to performing in theatre workshops.

2023 — Joining Christ

Joined the L3 Cohort

Christ University · Bangalore, Karnataka

Joined the MBA programme at Christ University as part of the prestigious L3 (Lean Operations and Systems) department — beginning a year of intense learning, friendships, and growth.

2024 — USHUS National Fest

Organising Team — USHUS
