The Journey

Year in Review

Every milestone, every event, every late night — in the order they happened.

Milestone

August 2023

Arrived in Bangalore

Left Karur and the factory floor behind — temporarily. Moved to Christ University to begin the MBA in Lean Operations & Systems. First time living away from home, first time in a truly campus-driven environment.

Academics

Sep – Nov 2023

MBA Semester 1 — L3 Cohort

Joined the L3 (Lean Operations & Systems) cohort. Supply chain deep-dives, case studies, and discovering that lean thinking applies just as much to a classroom project as a shop floor. Built friendships that lasted the whole year.

Events

October 2023

Pioneer Club — First Event

Became part of the Pioneer Club, Christ's student-run events collective. Helped organise the very first Pioneer event — from stage setup to audience coordination.

Leadership

Jan – Mar 2024

USHUS — National B-School Fest

Joined the organising team for USHUS, Christ University's flagship national business school festival. Managed logistics, vendor coordination, and on-ground execution for one of South India's biggest B-school events.

Community

June 2024

Back to School Community Initiative

Participated in a community outreach initiative — bringing MBA students back into school settings to mentor and connect with younger students.

Personal Dev

June 2024

Theatre Workshop

Took part in an inter-departmental theatre workshop in the last week of June. Cross-functional team building through performance — understanding communication, presence, and collaboration in a completely different context.

Academics

July 2024

OBT — Outbound Training

TVW cohort outbound training session. Team-building exercises, outdoor challenges, and the kind of bonding that only happens when you take people out of classrooms and put them through something physical together.

Competition

October 2024

Chrizellenz 2024 — 3rd Prize

Competed in the Business Analytics competition at Chrizellenz 2024, Christ University's annual inter-college fest. Placed 3rd — applying data analytics skills built on factory floors to a competitive academic setting.
